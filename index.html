Search

Search by Server: Rathnir, Eldham, Abexilas, Kanahulu,

You can search for any words in the book title. Additionally you can search for specific servers and authors (currently only signees):

  • :server:Rathnir to only show books that were available during Rathnir,
  • :signee:SomethingSaucy to only show books signed by SomethingSaucy.

In the future, full text search, search by topic, genre, original author, time of writing, current ingame library availability, Lawa Nasin, etc. may become available.

Pro tip: If you put just a as search query, all books are shown. This quirk works because all Stoneworks servers have an a in their name, even Eldham.
